I ran track in middle school and cross country in high school, but I was a terrible runner! As an adult, I signed up for a half marathon on a whim, thinking, “I hope my dad has enough tricks up his coaching sleeve to help me finish!”
With my dad's personalized 16-week training program, I went from running middle distances at an 11-minute pace to being able to run 10 miles (without stopping!) at a 9:30 pace. And I'm still getting faster!
I was one of those people who couldn't understand why anyone would wake up at 7 am on a Sunday to run. But now I get it, because I'm one of those people. I completely fell in love with running because of my dad's “start small” and “doable” coaching plan. Throughout training I never felt like I wouldn't be able to complete my half marathon, or finish the mile in front of me. Completing my race has been my biggest physical accomplishment to date. Thanks, Dad!
I now know at least two more things: Coach Wayne knows best, and I love being a runner.”
