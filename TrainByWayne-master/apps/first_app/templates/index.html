Meet Your Coach


I've run over 40,000 miles since 1974.


I'm a certified coach through USA Track and Field and the Road Runners Clubs of America. I began coaching at the high school level in 1987 and since then I've coached adults, kids, college athletes, my own children, and anyone else looking to become a runner.

I can help you achieve a healthier lifestyle through running. My specialty in coaching is taking non-runners and making them into runners and helping them to realize their potential. I can’t make you fast (that’s genetic), but I can make you faster. I can help you realize the health (physical and mental) benefits of being a runner. Running is not just about becoming a better runner, but also about becoming fitter, happier with who you are, taking care of yourself and sharing that positive outlook on life with others.

  • I've run in 5 different decades over 44 years.
  • I've had very few lay-offs; I'm on my 3rd knee (one wore out) and within 17 lbs of my weight from 35 yrs ago.
  • I've competed in everything from 200 meter races all the way to the Boston Marathon (twice) and most distances in between.
  • I've run on 4 different continents in 15 different countries, in -18 Fahrenheit to 125 Fahrenheit temperatures. I've run in almost every kind of weather including falling snow on trails (which is my favorite).
