Meet Your Coach


I started running from my 4 brothers when I was 3 and haven't finished yet. Organized running began in my early teens and I'm still at it 44 years later with very few lay-offs.

I've competed in everything from 200 meter races all the way to the Boston Marathon (twice) and most distances in between. I've run in 5 different decades on 4 different continents in 15 different countries.

I'm on my 3rd knee (one wore out) and within 17 lbs of my weight from 35 yrs ago. I've run in -18 Fahrenheit to 125 Fahrenheit temperatures, I've run in almost every kind of weather including falling snow on trails (which is my favorite). I've run over 40,000 miles total and started coaching in 1987 at the high school level. Since then I've coached adults, kids, college athletes, my own children, and anyone else looking to become a runner.

I'm a certified coach through USA Track and Field, and the Road Runners Clubs of America. I specialize in first time marathoners, but focus on making people of all ages runners for life.
